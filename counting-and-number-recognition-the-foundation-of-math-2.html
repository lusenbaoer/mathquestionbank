  1. Recognizing Numbers and Number Order 1.1 Numbers 0–9

Numbers are the key to understanding the world. From 0 to 9, each number has its own shape and meaning.

例子讲解 Example: Number 15 has 1 ten and 5 ones。数字 15 有 1 个十和 5 个一。
