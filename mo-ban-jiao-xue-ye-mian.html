What is a Fraction?

A fraction represents part of a whole. Example:

1/2 = one half
3/4 = three quarters
2/3 = two thirds

Numerator and Denominator

The top number is the numerator, the bottom is the denominator.

3/5 → 3 is numerator, 5 is denominator
7/8 → 7 is numerator, 8 is denominator

Adding Fractions

Fractions with the same denominator:

1/4 + 2/4 = 3/4
3/8 + 1/8 = 4/8 = 1/2

Understanding Decimals

Decimals are fractions written with a denominator of 10, 100, etc.

0.5 = 5/10 = 1/2
0.25 = 25/100 = 1/4

Basics of Probability

Probability measures how likely an event is to happen.

Coin toss: P(Heads) = 1/2
Rolling a die: P(6) = 1/6
