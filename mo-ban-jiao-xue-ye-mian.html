What is a Fraction?

A fraction represents a part of a whole.

  • 1/2 = One part out of two
  • 3/4 = Three parts out of four
  • 7/10 = Seven parts out of ten

Types of Fractions

Learn different fraction types.

  • Proper fraction: numerator smaller than denominator (3/4)
  • Improper fraction: numerator larger than denominator (7/4)
  • Mixed number: whole number plus fraction (1 3/4)

Equivalent Fractions

Fractions that represent the same value.

  • 1/2 = 2/4 = 4/8
  • 3/5 = 6/10 = 9/15
  • 2/3 = 4/6 = 6/9

Understanding Numerator

The top part of a fraction represents how many parts we have.

  • In 3/4, numerator = 3
  • In 7/10, numerator = 7
  • In 1/2, numerator = 1

Understanding Denominator

The bottom part shows into how many parts the whole is divided.

  • 1/4 → 4 equal parts
  • 3/10 → 10 equal parts
  • 7/8 → 8 equal parts

Adding Fractions

Fractions with same denominator:

  • 1/4 + 2/4 = 3/4
  • 3/8 + 2/8 = 5/8
  • 2/5 + 1/5 = 3/5

Subtracting Fractions

Fractions with same denominator:

  • 3/4 - 1/4 = 2/4
  • 5/8 - 2/8 = 3/8
  • 4/5 - 1/5 = 3/5

Real-life Fractions

Fractions appear in daily life.

  • Pizza slices: 3/8 of a pizza
  • Measuring cups: 1/2 cup of milk
  • Time: 3/4 hour of class
